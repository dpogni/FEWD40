chak·ra


ˈCHäkrə/
noun
(in Indian thought) each of the centers of spiritual power in the human body, usually considered to be seven in number.
  • Sahasrara or crown chakra
  • Anja or third-eye chakra
  • Vishuddha or throat chakra
  • Anahata or heart chakra
  • Manipura or navel chakra
  • Svadhishthana or sacral chakra
  • Muladhara or root chakra
